Dosa

Ice cream is a frozen dessert typically made from milk or cream that has been flavoured with a sweetener, either sugar or an alternative, and a spice, such as cocoa or vanilla, or with fruit, such as strawberries or peaches.

Ingredients

  • Strawberries
  • Milk
  • cream
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla
  • Food Coloring

Steps

  1. Combine all the ingredients in a large bowl
  2. Transfer the mixture to the freezer bowl of an ice cream maker
  3. Freeze accordingly
  4. Transfer the ice cream to an airtight container and freeze until firm
